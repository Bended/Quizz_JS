    TIP'S FOR YOU
  • Eat a high-fiber diet (wheat, corn, rice bran).
  • Exercise regularly, go to gym 2-3 times a week.
  • Limit foods that are high in fat, choose lean meats and eat on schedule.
  • Manage stress, since too much stress or anxiety can cause your digestive system to go into overdrive.
  • Stay hydrated. Water in your digestive system helps dissolve fats and soluble fiber, allowing these substances to pass through more easily.
